F.A.Q.

What is GetPly?

GetPly is Language Mastery Software (LMS) – innovative platform that allows any native speaker to create and sell unique online courses for any language, level or topic. You might even be so cheeky (we are!) as to call it “crowdcoursing.” The resulting collection of language mastery courses becomes a marketplace where students can find the courses that best fit their personality and learning goals.

What is Language Mastery Software?

The ultimate goal – the whole point – of completing a language course is to become fluent. Why is this a big deal to us? Because most language students will never achieve fluency. They become caught up in completing assignments or reading a textbook instead of doing the activities that actually lead to language fluency.

Perhaps most damaging to a student’s potential is the popular lie that fluency can be achieved with just a few minutes’ practice each day.

Even some of the most intelligent and dedicated students won’t become fluent in a second language if they aren’t given the right tools to succeed. Most training courses, whether they are designed to teach Spanish or needlepoint, focus on one type of approach, such as flashcards, multimedia, audio and so on. Each of them can take you a long way, but none of them alone will help you make the final leap to fluency. For that, it takes a more skilful, well-rounded approach to course creation.

We at GetPly have developed a new kind of software – Language Mastery Software, or LMS, that is based on a combination of several methodologies, from flashcards and audio to games and oral drills. The software is based on the latest practical applications from master teachers, cognitive neuroscience and psychology of learning.

It takes real time and genuine dedication to master a language...but here’s the good news: The GetPly method is as simple as it is effective. You will spend your time learning in the most effective way possible...and you will have fun doing it!

How is GetPly different from other e-learning platforms?

E-learning platforms like Teachable, Coursera, Udemy and hundreds of others all blur into one. They do one thing, and one thing only: replicate the classroom experience. These sites allow you to view text, videos and audio files from anywhere so you don’t need to commute – or even put on pants – to take a class.

Unfortunately, the e-learning technology didn’t live up to its promise. These platforms became glorified information products, based more on the publishing business model than anything else. The reality of this failure is reflected in the commoditization of the courses such sites offer and their falling prices.

Language mastery is not about receiving the material. You can get the pertinent information from a book or from an online class – there isn’t a difference. Language mastery is about spending hours of time one-on-one with a language, drilling, practicing and repeating the material.

GetPly courses use a set of tools that let you practice until the knowledge becomes automatic. And, you can practice anywhere. At the computer, on your phone, while walking, running, doing laundry, driving, waiting at the bus stop, sitting in the coffee shop or between classes.

In terms of course creation, GetPly takes the guesswork and long hours out of putting together learning materials. Anyone with creativity and the motivation to share their knowledge can build a language mastery course simply by following our step-by-step formula.

How is GetPly different from 1-on-1 teaching exchanges?

There is no shortage of student-teacher connection services, including Italki, HelloTalk and Tutor.com, plus a large number of teacher directories for Skype lessons. These are essentially offering online versions of a traditional – and antiquated – approach. The problem is that it doesn’t work. If it did, then everyone on planet Earth would speak two, three or more languages.

Language mastery takes more than conversation and coaching. We all know students who take classes for months and years and aren’t getting any closer to fluency than when they started.

For many students, taking classes or working with a language tutor becomes a form of procrastination. These lessons fill your need to work towards a goal, but they can be awkward to fit into a busy schedule and in the long run, they won’t make you fluent.

When you learn with GetPly, you can practice for a few minutes at a time and before you know it, a few minutes here and there add up to hours of valuable practice – without giving up any of your other priorities.
